Official Flag of Peru

Peru, located in South America, is a country known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse geography, and ancient civilizations. The capital city is Lima. Geographically, Peru is characterized by three main regions: the coastal desert, the Andean highlands, and the Amazon rainforest. Each region offers unique landscapes and ecosystems, from the arid coastal plains to the towering peaks of the Andes mountains and the lush biodiversity of the Amazon basin. Peru is renowned for its archaeological sites, including the famous Inca citadel of Machu Picchu, which attracts visitors from around the world. The country's history is also marked by pre-Columbian civilizations such as the Inca, Moche, and Nazca, whose legacies are preserved in ancient ruins and artifacts.
